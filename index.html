Final Fantasy Memory Card Game

Put your brain to work and match pairs of cards to eliminate them all. You will have 90secs to match all pairs in order to secure a win.

If you are able to match all pairs in 90secs, your name will be displayed on the ScoreBoard.

You can invite friends and initiate a friendly competition, or you can try to beat your best score.

!!! No point on trying to outsmart the game, every new round features a random shuffle!

Ready to put your brain to work? Input your name and hit Start!
